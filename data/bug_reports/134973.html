Comment 7 John Arthorne 2006-04-05 10:03:44 EDT 
This sounds mainly like a bug concerning the parsing behaviour of the ini file. It shouldn't require a new line to separate arguments, and it should ignore extra whitespace around arguments. It should simply treat arbitrary amounts of whitespace (new lines, space, tab) as argument delimiters.  Also, the file should support comments (lines starting with #) so that we can add comments to eclipse.ini describing its purpose and giving a pointer to the documentation of available arguments.

Comment 9 Pascal Rapicault 2006-04-05 10:54:53 EDT 
Splitting on white spaces would either force people to espace white spaces when they are actually needed (ie: Program files) or use delimiters.
Moreover that would likely force everybody to change their file, etc...
Basically I don't see that happening.
For background on the ini file, see bugs #76855, #80737 and follow ups.
I opened bug #135038 to track the necessity to support comments on the ini file.
