I did not realize the schema would end up being that complex, but it seems that it is required.

A few questions/comments:
- Management of version and version range is not consistent across the whole schema. Also you sometimes have repetitions (version as a varchar and version as a reference). Why?

We have a table called MetadataRepo, why is it necessary since the schema represent the metadata repository?


IUs/ fragments
- Maybe IU fragments could be folded back into the IUs table to simplify the schema and the request. If you don't fold it then you have to do a join for every IU lookup.
