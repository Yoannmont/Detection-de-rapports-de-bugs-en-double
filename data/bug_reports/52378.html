Comment 3 Nick Edgar 2004-02-23 12:13:19 EST 
This was a result of the new look merge (revision 1.30 of ViewPane, dated Feb 
13 2004).
Not sure why this was done.  Possibly for the progress indicator icon, but that 
went in later.  Maybe just due to the change in views no longer having a 
titlebar.  But the icon in the tab and/or fast view bar should still be updated.

We also need support for showing a modified view title, but I believe this is 
being introduced into this week's build using new widgetry.

classes that contain PartPanes to use a common implementation. The new and
improved fast view bar will share most of its code with view folders and editor

notifications).

are still on track for M9, although it will probably be another week before it
gets resolved.
