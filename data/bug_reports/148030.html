Comment 25 Onno van der Straaten 2009-01-15 14:32:43 EST 
Here are the command line switches, it took me some time to find them so I thought it would be useful to attach them to this bugzilla.
Required:\n\

\t-libraryPath <path to method library>\n\

\t-publishPath <path to publish to>\n\

\t-config <configuration to select from library>\n\

Optional:\n\

\t-about <about html file>\n\

\t-banner <banner image>\n\

\t-bannerHeight <banner image height>\n\

\t-checkLinks\n\

\t-dynamic <name>\n\

\t-feedback <feedback url>\n\

\t-glossary\n\

\t-index\n\

\t-publishUnverifiedDiagrams\n\

\t-title <title for published pages>
