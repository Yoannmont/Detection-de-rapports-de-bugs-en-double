Comment 18 Billy Biggs 2004-05-14 13:23:07 EDT 
I suspect the problem is in DefaultPartPresentation's showList() method where
we're listening for the deactivate and not getting it.  Haven't solved it yet
though.  It happens a lot but it's hard to get reproducable steps.

Regarding the maximize, you may also want to also see bug 59473.  Also,
sometimes you seem to be able to get multiple drop downs open at once, so you
might want to try clicking multiple times to get rid of it.
Comment 19 Billy Biggs 2004-05-21 12:46:01 EDT

seem to make it easier to reproduce.
