CoInternetIsFeatureEnabled was introduced in IE6 for XPSP2, so it is being detected and invoked on your machine as expected.  However my guess is that it's failing for you as a result of the constant that's being passed to it, which was introduced in IE7.  This should be an acceptable thing to do (it just results in a no-op), and I've verified that this happens on the IE6's around here.  However I could not find an IE6 here with the same version string that you have, so I could not replicate the problem, and I suspect that this is a bug in your IE version.

Attached is a patch that results in CoInternetIsFeatureEnabled and its related functions only being invoked if IE7 or later is detected.  IE7 is required for this code to make a functional difference anyways.
