Comment 7 Eclipse Webmaster 2005-07-05 16:03:37 EDT 
Wolfgang,

I noticed our mailing lists are not yet in gmane, so I just configured my
newsreader to news.gmane.org and subscribed to a couple of other eclipse-related
groups.  I noticed that the newsgroups are open to all, and that e-mail
addresses are not mangled in any way.  Is this true?  If so, I can understand
why the Eclipse Webmaster would have denied access to eclipse.org lists some
time ago. 

At Eclipse we have invested significant effort in achieving a compromise between
having open archives and SPAM protection. Our HTML-based archives are currently
open to the general public, but all the e-mail addresses, in the header and in
the body, get mangled by the archiving software before being published to HTML.

In other words, if gmane cannot reproduce what we have here at Eclipse.org, i.e.
mangling all e-mail addresses, then I'd prefer our lists not appear on gmane.
