The crash occurred in:
- eclipse 3.0 under java-sdk version 1.4.2, every update since release, on startup
- eclipse 3.0, 3.0.1 and 3.0.2 under java-sdk version 1.5.0 and 1.5.0_02 and
could be reproduced by "Window -> Preferences -> Workbench -> Compare/Patch"
- eclipse 3.1M7 under java-sdk version 1.5.0 and 1.5.0_02 (I don't have the
appropriate machine available, and I cannot remember how to reproduce it. I will
amend this as soon as possible.)

the latest gtk-qt-engine patch, since it cause problems with Firefox.

I reported this crash to Sun who replied:
"The stack trace indicates that the failure may be occurring in SWT native
code. Please ask Eclipse to investigate this issue further.  Since Eclipse
appears to trigger this failure, the developers of Eclipse will be able to
examine their product, isolate the cause of the behavior, and, if necessary,
provide us with a detailed report."

I will attach the crash report under 1.4.2 and 1.5.0 as well as an Email from
Sun which should contain useful information.
