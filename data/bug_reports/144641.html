Comment 8 Cameron Bateman 2006-06-02 13:30:01 EDT 
Thanks Lumir,

I now have a better handle on the problems you are seeing.

1) There are several syntax errors in test.jsp that prevent our features from working.  Unfortunately, the JSP editor does not flag these errors, although we are monitoring a number of bugs against that issue.  Try changing line 15 of your test.jsp to:

<f:view locale="#{loginBean.locale}">

This should solve your loadBundle problems.

The dataTable problem is a known issue -- we have not implemented symbol contribution for dataTable's yet.
