Comment 17 Jason Cwik 2008-05-08 10:34:28 EDT 
While I still think this is the most annoying "feature" ever, **did not exist in older versions of eclipse**, and is a usability problem, I think I've found a temporary workaround.

Right-click on your eclipse.exe executable and select properties. Under the compatability tab, Check "Run this program in compatibility mode" for Windows XP Service Pack 2.  Voilà!  No more stupid horizontal scrolling.

Note that this also works for other Eclipse 3.3-based programs like Adobe Flex Builder 3.
