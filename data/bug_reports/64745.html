Comment 3 John Arthorne 2004-05-31 12:06:12 EDT 
This bug is not about the lack of information, but about the fact that the
dialog never closed. The stack traces show that nothing is waiting any more, but
the dialog is still open. This is either a bug in core (failing to call

JM

Comment 5 Tod Creasey 2004-05-31 14:27:01 EDT 
We have a replicable case using the jobs view

1) Start a job that locks the workspace
2) Touch workspace more than once.

The problem here is that I am not nesting progress monitors properly. We 
should fix this for RC2 as it is serious but not that easy to get.
Comment 6 John Arthorne 2004-05-31 14:27:38 EDT 
Exact steps to reproduce:

 - Load org.eclipse.ui.examples.job
 - Open the Job Factory view
 - Check "Lock the workspace"
 - Click "Create Jobs"
 - Change quantity to 3 and duration to 10 seconds
 - Click "Touch Workspace"

-> After background job completes, dialog will stay hung forever
