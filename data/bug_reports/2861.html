Comment 10 John Stevenson 2006-03-30 12:27:36 EST 
Here are some related requests:

#1 There shall be the option to assign an icon to each menu entry. This icon shall be either pre-defined (and pre-assigned) or user definable (and user assigned).
 
#2 It shall be entirely user-selectable which icons will be displayed on the main icon bar. This selection shall be possible on icon-by-icon base, i.e. not only by enabling or disabling groups of icons (toolbars).
 
#3 The order of the icons in the iconbar shall be user-selectable. The ordering shall be possible on icon-by-icon base, i.e. not only by moving groups of icons (toolbars).
 
#4 It shall be possible to fully customize the menus. More specifically both the top-level menu structure (entries and their order), the type and order of the sub-entries within a top-level entry and the shortcut associated with each sub-entry shall be user-definable.
 
#5 It shall be possible to extend the pre-defined menus and their commands with user-defined commands. These commands will typically execute user scripts.
 
#6 It shall be possible to associate user-defined icons with user-defined scripts.
 
#7 It shall be possible to load and store the menu and icon configuration
