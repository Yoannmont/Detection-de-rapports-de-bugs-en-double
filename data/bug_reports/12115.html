Description Johan Walles 2002-03-22 09:38:05 EST

they most want to see fixed / implemented.

Comment 25 Johan Walles 2003-02-19 14:55:12 EST 
Nikolay, sorting by # votes is not obvious, but it is supported:

1. Fill in the query page.  Make sure to specify 1 for "Only bugs with at least:
   __ votes".  Launch your query.
2. At the bottom of the results page, click "Change Columns".
3. Check "votes" and press "Submit".
4. Now you have a votes column.  Click its header ("Votes") to sort by # votes.
