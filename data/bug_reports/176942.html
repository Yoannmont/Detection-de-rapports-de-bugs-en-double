Comment 2 Anton Leherbauer 2007-04-24 03:28:05 EDT 
The CDT editor does not use native code which could corrupt the JVM. Therefore, the JVM crash is either due to a bug in the JVM itself or in any of the referenced native libraries. It may also be a SWT bug.
Have you tried editing without CDT installed?
