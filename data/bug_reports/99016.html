Comment 1 Billy Biggs 2005-06-08 16:22:34 EDT 
There's really two problems.  When you click on the combo box's drop down, the
Combo is getting a FocusOut event, which is causing it to be destroyed. 
Destroying a Combo in FocusOut causes the crash you see.

The crash is reported as bug 98845, and so I am going to mark this as a
duplicate of that bug.  Bug 98283 is open regarding the loss of focus to the
Combo, which makes it awkward for using as a table editor.
