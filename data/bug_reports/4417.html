Comment 18 Steve Northover 2003-04-23 09:09:52 EDT 
Created attachment 4681 [details]
SWTInSWT ("works" on Motif only, tested on Linux)

Embedding one SWT in another SWT within the same process is similar to
embedding AWT in SWT.  In the example, the "Inner SWT" is created from a handle
