I am able to reproduce this in the 1.5.5 and 2.0 streams.  I found that I need to:
1. Put WTP under the "Program Files" directory.
2. Use the Windows explorer and double click on eclipse.exe to launch (only needed in 1.5.5, I was able to reproduce the problem in WTP 2.0 launching with the command line)

To resolve the problem, instead of converting a bundle URL to localURL, we can go straight to openStream on a bundleURL and pass that to the StreamSource.  I tested this in both 1.5.5 and 2.0, which fixes the problem.
