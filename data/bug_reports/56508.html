Comment 5 Avi Drissman 2004-06-02 10:03:04 EDT 
Some commentary from my dup bug 62513:

The tab isn't missing; it's just not rendered. This seems to happen when previous characters turn out to 
be a multiple of the tab width, and real tabs are enabled. My settings are 4 spaces/tab.

If we assume that the original reporter's settings are either 4 or 8 spaces/tab, then the example they 
give triggers the problem because "private FormText" is exactly 16 characters long, and then the tab 
falls right on a column of a multiple of the tab width.

See attachment 10740 [details] on my bug for a visual example.

While this is technically a display bug, it makes it difficult to work with Eclipse. Not only do columns 
just not line up, there are times that the code looks wrong but is really just fine.
