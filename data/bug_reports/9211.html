Comment 8 John Arthorne 2002-02-11 17:51:16 EST 
There were two problems here:

1) his VM was returning "i386" instead of the standard "x86" for the system 
property "os.arch".  This caused the initial UnsatisfiedLinkError exception.  
Core now handles i386 and maps it to x86, which should eliminate this first 
problem.

2) The second problem is the GPF in the KDE native code.  Moving to SWT 
component for further investigation.  I'm also updating the title to reflect 
this second error.
