Comment 2 Richard Kulp 2006-07-05 10:46:37 EDT 
I isn't JEM as far as I can tell. The problem is that an EMFContext (EMFWorkbenchContextBase) was created and the URI converter was changed from the default WorkbenchURIConverter that EMFWorkbenchContextBase.createURIConverter returns and EMFWorkbenchContextBase.initializeResourceSet sets into the the ProjectResourceSetImpl, and was set with a standard URIConverter instead. JEM doesn't do that. Some other extension of the EMF natures would of done it.
