Comment 8 Ian Stewart 2005-03-14 19:44:39 EST 
Ok, I've moved my Windows swap space to another partition with 1.6Gb free space.
RSSOwl no longer crashes Windows when I close tabs and I no longer get errors
about USER.EXE crashing etc. My conclusion is that some of the problems I was
experiencing were most likely due to insufficient swap space. My humble
apologies for any time I caused to be wasted or any false alarm I caused.

However, I still get a highly repeatable Windows crash when I run Eclipse
I20050222-081 and start closing tabs. However, no matter how many tabs I open
and close in Eclipse 3.1 M4 (using the exact same workspace and projects as
before) Windows never crashes - I just simply can't make it happen. Ever.

With I20050222-081 when it crashes I sometimes get the blue screen of death "A
fatal exception 0E has occurred at 018F:BFF9DFF". When I press the enter key to
continue the screen is replaced by a vertical pinstripe pattern and the system
is dead.

My firm belief is that something has changed in SWT to do with closing tabs
between Eclipse releases 3.1 M4 and 3.1 M5a that is causing Windows 98 to crash. 
(I also think it is high time I upgrade to a new machine...)
