Comment 3 David Williams 2005-10-04 13:02:28 EDT 
I suspect this isn't really hung. Just one huge performance problem with format
(for which there's several bugs open already). 

You can verify by putting break point at beginning of
JobSafeStructuredDocument.replaceText 
and hitting resume. 

I suspect its being called over and over while it slowly modifies the document.

Comment 7 David Williams 2005-10-19 01:38:56 EDT 
Just to document what's known so far, I think this is a problem in
EMF2DOMSSERenderer, 
setBatch method. 
If setBatch is passed a 'true' parameter, 
a call is made to "aboutToChange" method (thus acquiring the lock), 
but no call is ever made to "changed" method (to release it). 

I'll work with Chuck on what is most appropriate fix.
