This has been a lot of fun for me. However, I'm a little concerned that the
discussion heading in a direction that may be offensive to some folks and ask
that we bring it back to more of a make-fun-of-Wayne tone. I especially
appreciate that at least some folks think that I look young enough to be a
college student... I should also take this opportunity to make it known that I
am indeed wearing a bathing suit that is tied up nice and tight to avoid a
repeat of the first year's "crack" incident...

It's probably past time to provide a little background. Every year for the past
9 years, on the first day of January, a group of my friends and I head down to
the Rideau River (specifically, we go to Mooney's Bay), cut a hole in the ice
and then jump in one-by-one. We do this for two reasons: (1) because it's
actually a lot of fun and (2) to raise money for the local children's hospital
(CHEO). Last year, we managed to raise more than $5,000. This year, we're trying
to do even more.

I know that a lot of us are in the Ottawa area. If you want to participate this
year (and maybe yourself be the focus of next year's bug report) let me know.
You're welcome to join in on the fun!
